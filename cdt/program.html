    • About the program

    From the student’s viewpoint:

    • A student is expected to complete in 4 years
    • The first year is used to acquiring theoretical, technical, research and communication skills
      • Combination of lectures and project work.
      • Strong emphasis on group work
    • The student is a given a description of projects and supervisors, and selects 2 of these for short 10-week projects.
      • These are similar in scope to a master’s level project.
      • It is expected that one of these will go on to become the topic of the PhD
      • Key advantage: that the choice of substantive PhD research is
        1. not entirely open-ended; and
        2. delayed to a point where a student can make an informed choice about the project and supervisor.
